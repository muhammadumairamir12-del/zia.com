Our Story

Lahore's Property Experts Since 2016

SkyEagle Real Estate was founded in 2016 by Tariq Mehmood with a mission to bring honesty, professionalism, and innovation to Lahore's property market. What started as a small office in Gulberg has grown into Lahore's most awarded real estate consultancy, with a team of four expert agents and over 1,200 satisfied clients.

We specialize in residential, commercial, and investment properties across all of Lahore's premium zones — DHA, Bahria Town, Gulberg, Model Town, Wapda Town, Johar Town and more. Our commitment to verified listings, transparent dealing, and post-sale support sets us apart.
